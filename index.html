Origin

The One Ring is one of the most powerful artefacts ever created in Middle Earth.

It was crafted by the Dark Lord Sauron thousands of years ago in the fires of Mount Doom, during the Second Age. Sauron meant to use it to enhance his own power as well as to control all of the other rings of power.

Effects

Positive

  • Makes the wearer invisible to physical beings
  • Extends the lifespan of a mortal bearer
  • Sharpens one's senses
  • Grants the wearer some understanding of the speech of evil creatures

Negative

  • Makes the wearer highly visible to unseen beings, like Ringwraiths
  • Bearer is reluctant or unwilling to relinquish it
  • If worn too often, the wearer will become wraith-like over time
  • The ring has a will of its own, always trying to get back to Sauron
